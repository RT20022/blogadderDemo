About
M6 Automotive Pvt. Ltd.

M6 Automotive Services Private Limited is a leading name in the natural gas industry. We are proud to be an ISO-9001:2015 certified company, dedicated to delivering exceptional quality and service. As a registered start-up with the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) under Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Government of India. We are recognized as a Micro-industry under Udyam by the Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises. This recognition underscores our commitment to innovation and growth within the industry.

At M6 Automotive, we specialize in the testing of a wide range of cylinders, including natural gas and hydrogen cylinders. Our facility is equipped with the latest technology and advanced testing equipment, allowing us to ensure the highest standards of quality and reliability. We understand the critical role that safe and effective gas storage plays in various applications, and we are dedicated to providing thorough and accurate testing services. Our facility is approved by Petroleum and Explosives Safety Organisation (PESO), Government of India. The cylinder testing is fully compliant with regulatory requirements.

In addition to our testing capabilities, we offer a comprehensive suite of services related to gas cylinder cascades. Our expertise extends to the installation, uninstallation, repair, operation, maintenance, and transportation of gas cylinder systems. This holistic approach allows us to serve as your one-stop solution for all gas cylinder and cascade needs, simplifying the process for our clients and ensuring peace of mind.

Our team consists of highly trained technicians and experienced supervisors who are committed to maintaining the highest safety standards in all operations. We prioritize safety, efficiency, and compliance with industry regulations, ensuring that our services are conducted in a reliable manner. Our commitment to best practices not only safeguards our team and clients but also enhances the overall quality of our services.
At M6 Automotive Services, customer satisfaction is at the core of our mission. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients by providing exceptional service and support tailored to their specific needs. Whether you require testing, maintenance, or installation services, we are here to provide you with the finest quality and expertise in the industry.
