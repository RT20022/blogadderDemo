ULTRASONIC THICKNESS GAUGE

An ultrasonic thickness gauge is a non-destructive testing (NDT) device used to measure the thickness of walls of Cylinder.

BORESCOPE

A borescope is an optical inspection tool used to visually examine internal cavities, internal cracks and corrosion pits etc inside the cylinder using high resolution imaging technology.

ULTRASONIC FLAW DETECTOR

An ultrasonic flaw detector is a non-destructive testing method that employs high-frequency ultrasound waves to inspect materials and detect flaws or defects
